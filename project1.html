Fairy Garden Google Doodle

Faith, trust and GSAP Animations

Client Name: Google

Platform: Website

Time Frame: April 2021 - June 2021

Final Project

Project Description

This project was created using GreenSock animations and Illustrator SVG files. Using multiple simple animations, I was able to bring this Fairy Google Doodle to life.

Major Tasks & Responsibilities

Illustrating the parts for the google doodle, creating and styling the doodle with HTML and CSS, and animated paths with Javascript.

Project Goals

I wanted this google doodle to feel very earthy and magical. A big inspiration for it was the little miniature fairy houses and gardens that people will build and collect. While watching it, I wanted the user to feel like they are in a magical fairy land, surrounded by greenery, cute little mushrooms, and fairies flying all around.

Sketches

Illustrator Work

I began this project by creating the different pieces in Illustrator. Rverything needed to be seperate if I wanted different parts to move differently, at different times. The smaller dark green pieces were used to cover the vines to make it appear like they were wraping around the letters.

Project Challenges

At the time I created this project, I had very little knowledge of Javascript. The simple GSAP animations were the only thing I knew how to do. In my original plan I wanted the vines to grow up the letters "G" and "l". I was unable to do it using the GSAP animations, and I spent hours trying to figure out a solution. I look forward to continue to work on this and have my vision complete once I am more comfortable with Javascript.
